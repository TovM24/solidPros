Simple Steps to Convert Images into Editable Text

How to Upload Images on the Website

On the website, initiate the image uploading process by locating and clicking on the 'Upload Image' button.

After selecting an image, the website will automatically display a preview with optimized dimensions for convenient viewing.

Welcome to our website! We take pride in introducing the primary function of our site, which is Optical Character Recognition (OCR) technology.
